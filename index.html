Project Milestones

General overview of our time working together!

  • Phase One

    Onboarding and Strategy

    Initial survey and consultation expected to help us understand your company goals and target audience. We will work with you to establish a project timeline as well as present you with the drafted wireframes and site architecture specific to your company goals.

  • Phase Two

    Planning and Research

    From the technologies to be used, site structure and naming conventions, we will provide you with an effective content outline and strategy intended to improve your online presence and credibility as a business.

  • Phase Three

    Design

    During this process, a mock-up will be created for your approval to ensure components such as typography, images, color schemes, etc. coincide with your company identity.

  • Phase Four

    Development

    Once the drafted design and content is approved, the task of building your website can begin. One of the longest phases of the project is the development phase, this is where all the coding takes places!

  • Phase Five

    Testing and Deployment

    Before launch, the site will be placed on a production server where only in-house audiences and those who have been distributed the live link can view it. Your site will be deployed once all components of the site have been approved.
